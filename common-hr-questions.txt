COMMON HR INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why do you want to work with our company?
3. What are your strengths?
4. What is one area you are working to improve?
5. Why should we hire you?
6. Where do you see yourself in the next 3 years?
7. How do you handle pressure or deadlines?
8. Tell me about a challenge you faced and how you handled it.
9. Are you comfortable working in a team?
10. What motivates you at work?
11. Why are you leaving your current role?
12. What salary are you expecting?
13. Are you willing to relocate?
14. What do you know about this role?
15. Do you have any questions for us?

ATTOLIGENCE TIP:
Do not memorize answers word for word. Understand the intent of each question and answer naturally with examples.
