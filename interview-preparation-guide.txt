ATTOLIGENCE INTERVIEW PREPARATION GUIDE

1. Understand the role
- Read the job description carefully
- Note the required skills, tools, and responsibilities
- Prepare examples from your own education or work that match the role

2. Research the company
- Learn what the company does
- Understand the industry, products, and customers
- Be ready to explain why you want to join

3. Prepare your introduction
- Start with your name
- Mention your qualification or experience
- Highlight your strongest skills
- Connect your background to the role

4. Practice common interview questions
- Tell me about yourself
- Why do you want this job
- What are your strengths
- What is one area you are improving
- Why should we hire you

5. Use the STAR method for answers
- Situation
- Task
- Action
- Result

6. Improve communication
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Keep answers structured
- Avoid one-word answers
- Maintain confidence and professional body language

7. Prepare documents
- Updated resume
- Academic documents if required
- ID proof if required
- Portfolio or work samples if available

8. Final checklist
- Dress professionally
- Join early if it is an online interview
- Test microphone and camera
- Carry a notebook and pen
- Keep your phone on silent

ATTOLIGENCE TIP:
Confidence grows with preparation. Practice your answers, review your resume, and attend mock interviews before the real interview.
