ATTOLIGENCE CAREER ROADMAP

STEP 1: Identify your target role
- Choose the role you want to build toward
- Example: HR Recruiter, Sales Executive, Software Developer, Medical Representative

STEP 2: Understand role requirements
- Qualification
- Skills
- Communication level
- Industry expectations

STEP 3: Build core skills
- Technical or job-specific skills
- Communication and interview skills
- Resume and profile improvement

STEP 4: Gain practical exposure
- Internships
- Projects
- Workshops
- Mock interviews

STEP 5: Prepare job documents
- Resume
- Cover note if required
- LinkedIn profile
- Portfolio or project links

STEP 6: Start applying strategically
- Apply only to relevant roles
- Track each application
- Prepare before every interview

STEP 7: Improve through feedback
- Review interview performance
- Fix weak answers
- Improve confidence and clarity

STEP 8: Continue learning
- Take role-specific training
- Add certifications
- Stay updated with your industry

ATTOLIGENCE TIP:
Career growth happens faster when you combine skill building, practical preparation, and consistent application.
