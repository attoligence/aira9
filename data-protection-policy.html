Data Protection Policy

This policy outlines how Attoligence collects, processes, stores, and safeguards personal data.

Last Updated: 2026

Attoligence Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Attoligence", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the personal data of candidates, companies, students, and other individuals who interact with our services.

This Data Protection Policy outlines how Attoligence collects, processes, stores, and safeguards personal data while providing recruitment, training, and career development services.

By using our website and services, users acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Purpose of This Policy

The purpose of this Data Protection Policy is to ensure that Attoligence handles personal data responsibly and securely while providing services such as:

  • recruitment and talent acquisition
  • career development programs
  • training and skill development
  • mock interview sessions
  • workforce solutions for businesses

This policy also explains how Attoligence protects the privacy and rights of individuals whose information is processed through our platform.

2. Types of Data We Collect

Attoligence may collect and process the following types of personal data.

Candidate Information

Information provided by candidates may include:

  • full name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • educational background
  • work experience
  • professional skills
  • resume or curriculum vitae
  • career preferences

This information is used to help match candidates with job opportunities and career development programs.

Employer Information

Organizations using Attoligence services may provide:

  • company name
  • contact person details
  • business email and phone number
  • hiring requirements
  • job descriptions

This information is used to support recruitment and hiring processes.

Website Usage Data

When users visit the website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • browser type
  • device information
  • pages visited
  • usage behavior

This information helps improve website functionality and user experience.

3. How We Use Personal Data

Attoligence processes personal data for the following purposes:

  • connecting candidates with employers
  • providing recruitment services
  • organizing mock interview sessions
  • managing training and development programs
  • responding to user enquiries
  • improving platform functionality and services

Personal data may also be used to send relevant updates about:

  • job opportunities
  • training programs
  • career resources

Users may opt out of communications if they choose.

4. Data Storage and Security

Attoligence takes appropriate steps to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Security measures may include:

  • secure data storage systems
  • restricted access to sensitive information
  • monitoring and security controls
  • secure communication channels

While we implement strong safeguards, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Attoligence does not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

However, information may be shared in limited circumstances, including:

  • with companies seeking candidates for employment opportunities
  • with training partners conducting professional programs
  • with service providers assisting in website or platform operations
  • when required by law or government authorities

All partners are expected to handle personal data responsibly.

6. Data Retention

Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

Candidate profiles and resumes may be stored for future job matching unless the user requests removal.

Users may request deletion of their personal data at any time.

7. User Rights

Individuals have the right to:

  • access their personal data
  • request corrections to inaccurate information
  • request deletion of their data
  • withdraw consent for data processing

Requests can be submitted through the contact information provided below.

8. Data Protection Responsibilities

Attoligence is responsible for ensuring that personal data is:

  • collected fairly and lawfully
  • used only for legitimate purposes
  • stored securely
  • protected against unauthorized access

Employees and partners of Attoligence are expected to follow data protection practices when handling personal information.

9. Third-Party Platforms

Our website may contain links to external platforms such as job portals, training providers, or communication tools.

Attoligence is not responsible for the data protection practices of third-party websites.

Users are encouraged to review the policies of external platforms before sharing personal information.

10. Updates to This Policy

Attoligence may update this Data Protection Policy periodically to reflect changes in services or regulatory requirements.

The updated version will be published on our website with the revised date.
