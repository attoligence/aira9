Resume Writing Master Guide

Create a resume that gets shortlisted in seconds by combining ATS structure, strong relevance, and proof-driven achievement bullets.

Presented by Attoligence Technologies Pvt. Ltd. Resume Building. Interview Preparation. Placement Support.
Reality Check

How Recruiters Actually Read Your Resume

Average screening time is only 6 to 10 seconds. Recruiters do not read every line first. They scan for job title match, skills match, recent experience, achievements, and clean formatting.

Critical Insight

If your resume does not show relevance instantly, it is likely to be rejected before a full review even begins.

Resume Strategy

Your Resume Is a Marketing Document

It is not a biography. It should not be a long list of duties.

It is proof of value. It should answer one question clearly: why should this company hire you?

Perfect Structure

ATS and Recruiter Friendly Layout

Header

Name, phone, email, and LinkedIn. Avoid photo and full address.

Professional Summary

Who you are, your strongest skills, experience level, and goal in 3 to 4 lines.

Skills Section

Separate technical skills, tools, and soft skills. Match the job description keywords.

Experience / Projects

This is the core section. Show business value, contribution, and outcomes.

Education

Degree, college, year, and score. Keep it direct and clean.

Optional Value Sections

Certifications, internships, achievements, and languages can strengthen your profile.

High Impact Writing

Bullet Formula That Gets Attention

Each strong bullet should follow this structure: Action Verb + Task + Tool + Result.

Strong Example

Developed a web application using React, improving user engagement by 40%.

Weak Example

Worked on team projects.

Power Action Words

  • Developed
  • Led
  • Improved
  • Optimized
  • Designed
  • Implemented
ATS Optimization

Pass the System Before a Human Even Sees You

Use standard headings such as Skills, Experience, Education, and Certifications.

Use keywords directly from the job description wherever honestly relevant.

Avoid images, tables, icons, and decorative formatting that ATS may not read correctly.

Use simple fonts and save as a clean text-based PDF for final submission.

Candidate Type Strategy

Freshers vs Experienced Candidates

For Freshers

Focus on projects, internships, certifications, skills, and proof of ability. You do not need long experience to show value.

For Experienced Candidates

Focus on achievements, business impact, leadership, ownership, and measurable growth.

Common Rejection Reasons

Mistakes That Damage Resume Quality

Spelling and grammar errors

Generic one-resume-for-all-jobs approach

No achievements, only duties

Poor formatting and irrelevant information

Final Checklist

Before You Send Your Resume

  • Is it relevant to the job?
  • Is it ATS-friendly?
  • Does it show real achievements?
  • Is the formatting clean?
  • Are there zero spelling errors?
  • Have you saved the final version as PDF?

Final Insight

A strong resume does not get you the job. It gets you the interview. Interviews get you the job.
