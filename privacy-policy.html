Privacy Policy for Attoligence

Last Updated: 2026

Attoligence Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Attoligence", "we", "our", or "us") values the privacy of our users and is committed to protecting the personal information of candidates, companies, and visitors who interact with our website and services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when users access our website, services, or communicate with us.

By using the Attoligence website and services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect different types of information from users depending on how they interact with our platform.

Personal Information

Personal information may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Educational qualifications
  • Employment history
  • Resume or CV information
  • Professional skills and experience

This information may be collected when users:

  • Submit resumes
  • Apply for job opportunities
  • Register for mock interviews
  • Register for training programs
  • Submit enquiry forms
  • Contact us through the website

Business Information

Companies using our services may provide information such as:

  • Company name
  • Contact person details
  • Company contact information
  • Hiring requirements
  • Business industry details

Automatically Collected Information

When users visit our website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Website usage data
  • Pages visited

This information helps us improve website performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Attoligence may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing recruitment services
  • Connecting candidates with companies
  • Responding to enquiries and support requests
  • Managing training programs and mock interviews
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Communicating updates about our services

We may also use information to send notifications about:

  • Job opportunities
  • Training programs
  • Career development resources

Users can opt out of such communications at any time.

3. Sharing of Information

Attoligence does not sell personal information to third parties.

However, information may be shared in the following situations:

  • With companies seeking candidates for recruitment purposes
  • With training partners conducting learning programs
  • With service providers supporting our operations
  • When required by law or regulatory authorities

All partners and service providers are expected to handle information responsibly.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Security practices may include:

  • Secure data storage systems
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Monitoring systems to prevent unauthorized activity

Despite these measures, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data usage

Requests can be made by contacting Attoligence through the contact information provided on our website.

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience.

Cookies help us:

  • Understand user behavior
  • Improve website performance
  • Customize website content

Users may disable cookies through browser settings if preferred.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites or platforms.

Attoligence is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of external platforms before sharing personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Attoligence services are intended for individuals seeking career opportunities or professional services.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Attoligence may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When updates occur, the revised version will be posted on this page with the updated date.

Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
